**N=100 trials, seed=42. Three real agent archetypes.**

## Datasets

- **AgentBench KG** (Liu et al., ICLR 2024): 1364 sessions, 9378 total actions. GPT-4 KG traversal.
- **SWE-agent CTF** (Yang et al., NeurIPS 2024): 10 sessions, 109 total actions. CTF challenges. run_shell dominant (~45%).
